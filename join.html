The Book Verse

Where stories breathe and writers thrive.

Guideline 01

A community for readers and writers

This community is created for passionate readers and especially for writers who love creating books, poems, stories, and other forms of literature.

Guideline 02

Supportive environment

Our members believe in supporting one another. Everyone is encouraged to share ideas, feedback, and encouragement. Support others and expect the same respect in return.

Guideline 03

No political or religious debates

Political, religious, or any controversial discussions that may create conflict or harm the community environment are strictly not allowed.

Guideline 04

Focus on literature and creativity

Conversations should mainly focus on books, writing, poetry, storytelling, reading habits, publishing, and learning from one another.

Guideline 05

Respect for everyone

Every member must treat others with respect regardless of their religion, background, nationality, gender, or personal beliefs.

Guideline 06

Zero tolerance for discrimination

Any form of disrespect, discrimination, hate speech, racism, or harassment will not be tolerated and may result in immediate removal from the community.

Guideline 07

Positive and constructive interaction

Always communicate politely and provide constructive feedback when discussing someone’s writing or ideas.

Guideline 08

No spam or unrelated promotions

Avoid sharing spam, advertisements, or unrelated promotional content unless it is directly connected to books, writing, or reading.

Guideline 09

Protect intellectual work

Respect the originality of others’ work. Do not copy, repost, or claim someone else's writing without proper credit.

Guideline 10

Encourage learning and growth

Members are encouraged to share knowledge, book recommendations, writing tips, and resources that help others grow as readers and writers.

Guideline 11

Maintain a safe and friendly space

This community aims to remain a safe, friendly, and inspiring environment for everyone who loves literature.

Final Step

Agreement to follow the guidelines

By joining this community, you agree to respect and follow these guidelines to help maintain a healthy and inspiring space for all members.
