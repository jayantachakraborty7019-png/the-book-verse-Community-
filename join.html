Community Guidelines

Please review and acknowledge all points below

1. A community for readers and writers

This community is created for passionate readers and especially for writers who love creating books, poems, stories, and other forms of literature.

2. Supportive environment

Our members believe in supporting one another. Everyone is encouraged to share ideas, feedback, and encouragement.

3. No political or religious debates

Political, religious, or any controversial discussions that may create conflict or harm the community environment are strictly not allowed.

4. Focus on literature and creativity

Conversations should mainly focus on books, writing, poetry, storytelling, reading habits, and publishing.

5. Respect for everyone

Every member must treat others with respect regardless of their religion, background, nationality, gender, or personal beliefs.

6. Zero tolerance for discrimination

Any form of disrespect, hate speech, racism, or harassment will result in immediate removal.

7. Positive and constructive interaction

Always communicate politely and provide constructive feedback when discussing someone’s writing.

8. No spam or unrelated promotions

Avoid sharing spam or advertisements unless directly connected to books or writing.

9. Protect intellectual work

Respect the originality of others’ work. Do not copy or claim someone else's writing without proper credit.

10. Encourage learning and growth

Members are encouraged to share knowledge, recommendations, and writing tips.

11. Maintain a safe and friendly space

This community aims to remain an inspiring environment for everyone who loves literature.

12. Agreement to follow guidelines

By joining, you agree to help maintain a healthy and inspiring space for all members.
