Terms and Conditions

Our guidelines for a vibrant, creative community

Welcome to our sanctuary of words. Please read these terms carefully before spreading your wings in our community.

1 Acceptance of Terms

By joining and participating in this community, you agree to follow all the rules, guidelines, and policies established for maintaining a respectful and positive environment.

2 Purpose of the Community

This community exists to encourage reading, writing, and meaningful discussions related to books, literature, poetry, storytelling, and creative ideas.

3 Member Responsibilities

Members are expected to behave respectfully, communicate politely, and contribute positively to the community.

4 Respectful Communication

Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, racism, personal attacks, or offensive language toward any member will not be tolerated.

5 Content Sharing

Members may share their writings, book recommendations, opinions, and literary discussions. However, content that is offensive, harmful, illegal, or unrelated to the purpose of the community is not allowed.

6 Intellectual Property

Members must respect the ownership of creative work. Do not copy, distribute, or claim someone else’s writing, poems, or ideas without proper credit or permission.

7 No Harmful Discussions

Political debates, religious arguments, or any controversial discussions that may create conflict or negativity in the community are not permitted.

8 No Spam or Unauthorized Promotions

Spamming, advertising unrelated services, or excessive self-promotion without permission from the administrators is not allowed.

9 Administrator Rights

Community administrators reserve the right to moderate discussions, remove inappropriate content, warn members, or remove any member who violates the community rules.

10 Privacy and Safety

Members should respect the privacy of others and avoid sharing personal information of any member without their consent.

11 Changes to Terms

The community administrators may update or modify these terms and conditions when necessary to maintain a healthy and safe environment.

12 Agreement to Comply

By joining and continuing to participate in the community, members confirm that they understand and agree to follow these terms and conditions.
